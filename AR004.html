How to Use

  1. Upload a 3D model in GLTF, GLB, STL, or OBJ format
  2. Preview your model in the 3D viewer - you can rotate and zoom
  3. Adjust the AR scale if needed using the slider
  4. Click "View in AR" to see your model in augmented reality at 11m scale
  5. For AR viewing, point your camera at a flat surface with good lighting
  6. Move around to see your model from different angles

About the 11m Scale

This viewer is optimized for large-scale models up to 11 meters in size. This is ideal for:

  • Architectural models and buildings
  • Industrial equipment and machinery
  • Vehicles and large objects
  • Environmental designs and layouts

The scale slider allows you to adjust the size from 10% to 200% of the optimized 11m scale.
